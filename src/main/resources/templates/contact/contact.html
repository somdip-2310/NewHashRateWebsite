Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical response time?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Emergency support is available 24/7 with response times under 4 hours.

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes, we offer complimentary initial consultations to understand your requirements and provide preliminary recommendations.

What geographic areas do you serve?

We serve clients nationwide with local offices and certified partners in major metropolitan areas. International projects are also supported.

Can you work with existing systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in integrating new solutions with existing infrastructure and can retrofit or upgrade most legacy systems.

Do you provide training?

Yes, comprehensive training programs are included with all installations. We also offer ongoing education and certification courses.

What about warranty and support?

All installations include comprehensive warranties and we offer various support packages including 24/7 monitoring and maintenance services.
